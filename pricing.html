Frequently Asked

Every Sunday evening, 6 PM ET. Like clockwork. One email, everything that mattered in the markets that week, and what I'm watching for the week ahead.

Yes. No questions asked, no guilt trip, no "are you sure?" pop-ups. Click cancel and you're done. I'd rather you come back later than feel trapped.
